Almaty region will take part in KITF-2022

16th May, 2022

The Department of Tourism of the Almaty Region and the Tourist Information Center of the Almaty Region Zhetysu Travel jointly prepared a number of stands for the Kazakhstan International Exhibition KITF-2022.

To make this process as open and transparent as possible, representatives of the Tourism Department of the Almaty Region and Zhetysu Travel will take part in the Kazakhstan International Exhibition KITF, which will be held on April 20-22, 2022 at the Atakent Exhibition Center. KITF-2022 will feature a range of booths designed specifically to promote domestic tourism and its restart program in the region.

The main goal of participation in the Kazakhstan International Exhibition will be the promotion of the domestic tourism product. According to the region's tourism department, residents of Almaty and other regions of Kazakhstan are not sufficiently familiar with the opportunities that travel in their native land opens up for them, and especially tourism in the very heart of Central Asia - Semirechye.

Participants of the Kazakhstan International Exhibition KITF-2022 will be tour operators and travel agencies, airlines and air agencies, national and regional tourism organizations, accommodation facilities, medical and health centers, developers and suppliers of IT technologies in tourism, as well as companies representing real estate abroad and related services for tourism activities. Among the stands installed in the Atakent exhibition center, one can also find foreign representations: the Embassy of Indonesia, the Tourism Authority of Thailand, the Japan Tourism team.

Almaty region at KITF-2022 will be represented by the stands of five national parks, including the Ile-Alatau State National Natural Park, the Altyn-Emel State National Natural Park, the Charyn State National Natural Park, the Kolsai Lakes State National Natural Park and Zhongar- Alatau State National Natural Park. Also, organizations such as Altyn Adam Zhetisu Sheberler Ortalygy public association, Limon Travel and Jetysu Camping travel companies, as well as Alakol Resort hotels, Ak-Bulak mountain resort, Oi-Qaragai family and mountain resort will also take part in the presentation of the restart of the industry. .

Let's add that the Almaty region is the most picturesque region of Kazakhstan. High mountains and endless steppes, blue lakes and hot deserts make up the picturesque landscape of Zhetysu nature. In Kazakh, Zhetysu means seven rivers, and this place used to be a source of inspiration for many Kazakh poets. They called this place heaven on earth, and not without reason, because it is so different from other parts of Kazakhstan that it seems like an amazing oasis hidden in the Asian steppes.
