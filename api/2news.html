A major increase in the number of air passengers occurred in Kazakhstan

16th May, 2022

The number of passengers carried in the three months of 2022 increased by five percent compared to the same period in 2021. This was reported by the Civil Aviation Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Tengritravel.kz reports.

So, for three months of this year, two million passengers were transported by Kazakh airlines, and in the same period of 2021, the figure was 1.9 million people. In pre-pandemic 2020, the number of passengers was 17 percent less.

It is reported that the passenger flow of Air Astana amounted to about 800 thousand people, FlyArystan and SCAT - about 500 thousand passengers.

At the airports of the country for the specified period, the number of passengers served amounted to 3.7 million people, which is two percent more than last year (2021 - 3.6 million people, 2020 - 3.3 million people).

Currently, domestic flights are operated by five domestic airlines on 57 routes with a frequency of 613 flights per week. International flights are operated to 24 countries on 77 routes with a frequency of 319 flights per week. These are Turkey, UAE, Uzbekistan, Georgia, Tajikistan, Russia, Kyrgyzstan, Belarus, Germany, Egypt, South Korea, the Netherlands, Poland, Armenia, Great Britain, Maldives, Thailand, Azerbaijan, Qatar, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Mongolia, India and Iran .
