From which cities of Kazakhstan you can fly to Egypt

16th May, 2022

Egypt is one of the most popular holiday destinations for Kazakhstanis. Today, there are no direct regular flights to the country from Kazakhstan, so you can fly on charters by purchasing a tour package from operators. Tengritravel.kz, with reference to the Civil Aviation Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Kazakhstan and information provided by airlines, tells from which cities in Kazakhstan you can get to Egyptian resorts.

According to the Civil Aviation Committee, 12 flights a week are available from Kazakhstan to Egypt.

Nur-Sultan - Sharm el-Sheikh (Air Astana - 2, SCAT - 3).

Almaty - Sharm el-Sheikh (Air Astana - 3, SCAT - 3).

Atyrau - Sharm el-Sheikh; (Fly Arystan - 1).

According to Air Astana, the number of flights to Sharm el-Sheikh from Almaty has increased to three times a week since April 25.

The carrier Fly Arystan, as noted in the CAC, has no changes. There is one charter flight per week from Atyrau to Sharm El Sheikh. Previously , three charter flights to Egypt were available to Kazakhstanis : Atyrau - Sharm el-Sheikh (Fly Arystan - two) and Nur-Sultan - Sharm el-Sheikh (SCAT - one). Flights from Kazakhstan were resumed in early March 2022 after a three-month break due to the unfavorable epidemiological situation.
