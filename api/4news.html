KITF 2022: New tourism products and development opportunities for current destinations

16th May, 2022

From April 20 to April 22, 2022, a real holiday of tourism will be held in Almaty at the Atakent exhibition center - the Kazakhstan International Exhibition "Tourism and Travel" KITF 2022.

Since 2001, the KITF exhibition has been the largest industry b2b event in Central Asia. This year, about 200 companies from 17 countries have already confirmed their participation in the exhibition. In the anniversary year for the project, such companies as Antaal Life, EcoPark Tour, KOMPAS Touroperator, SCAT, AIR ASTANA, FUN & SUN Travel, Rustar, Selfie Travel, Fly Arystan, Cornelia Hotel, regional tourism departments from all over Kazakhstan announced their participation and many others.

Despite the logistical difficulties, the exposition will feature national stands of Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Georgia, Indonesia, Malaysia, Poland, South Korea, and Uzbekistan.

To date, the tourism industry in Kazakhstan is actively developing and, following the results of the past year, has a number of professional achievements. As a result of the work carried out, Kazakhstan won the nomination "Best Guest Houses" according to the global publishing house Lonely Planet. The French publication Le Figaro has included Kazakhstan in the list of 21 destinations recommended for visiting in 2021, the British Backpacker Society has identified Kazakhstan in the top 5 must visit list for 2020 for fans of active eco-travel. The Telegraph named Kazakhstan as a "fast-growing tourism destination" and Almaty as a top city budget destination for 2022.

Participation in the KITF exhibition is a promising solution for travel companies and travel agencies in neighboring countries. Visa-free regime with Kazakhstan, geographic proximity and lack of a language barrier can serve as objective reasons for the participation of companies from neighboring countries. At the KITF site, 100% coverage of the travel agency network of Kazakhstan takes place, and among the visitors of the exhibition there are tourism industry professionals from 39 countries, mainly from Central Asia.
